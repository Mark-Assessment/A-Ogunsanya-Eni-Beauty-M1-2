Biab

At Eni beauty you can get french tips of any colour of choice. The french tips are drawn free hand to get a perfect smile line. Biab means Builder gel In A Bottle. It is totally for you if your nails are weak and break easily and if you are looking to let your natural nails grow. It will bring protection and strength to your nails. The biab is applied on your natural nails and after 2-3 weeks, you will need to get an infill done.

Soft gel tips

Have you ever tried the soft gel tips? The most natural look as extensions. Press on nails

You can also get press on nails with the design of your choice. You can choose this option for special occasions Firstly, we book an appointment and discuss about the design and shape that you want and this first appointment will allow us to know the size of your nails to get the correct tips and to know when it needs to be ready. We will then contact you to let you know that your press on nails are ready, then an appointment will be necessary to help you to put them on or you can just collect them and we will provide you glue stickers. the press on nails are reusable. It can last few days, to take them off, simply soak your hands in warm ater to melt the glue stickers.
